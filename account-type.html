Insta.Standard Trading Accounts

Insta.Standard is a versatile trading account denominated in US dollars (USD), suitable for traders of all experience levels. The minimum deposit is just 1 USD, with no maximum limit. Spreads start from 0.00006 pips, and there are no trading commissions.

Trade sizes range from 0.01 to 10,000 lots with market order execution. Leverage is adjustable from 1:1 up to 1:1000, allowing for flexible strategies. Margin levels are set at 25% for local margin, 30% for Margin Call, and 10% for Stop Out. This account is suitable for Micro Forex (from 1 USD), Mini Forex (from 100 USD), and Standard Forex (from 1,000 USD), providing flexibility and risk control.

Cent.Standard Trading Accounts

Cent.Standard is a cent-denominated trading account tailored for beginners and those looking to test strategies with minimal risk. The account is in USD cents, enabling extremely low-volume trades with minimal costs.

Trade sizes start from 0.10 cent lots (equivalent to 0.0001 of a standard lot in market terms). The minimum deposit is just 1 USD, with no upper limit. Trading conditions include spreads from 0.00006 pips, no commissions, and market order execution.
Leverage ranges from 1:1 to 1:1000. Capital protection features include a 25% local margin, 30% Margin Call, and 10% Stop Out levels.
The Cent.Standard account is perfect for safe learning, algorithm tuning, and strategy testing in real-market conditions with minimal deposit stress.
